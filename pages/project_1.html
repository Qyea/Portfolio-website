Why that?

The project was developed by students of the 2nd year of the BSU FPMI as part of the course â€œProgramming Technologies". The goal of this project is to create a social network that will allow users to communicate with each other, share news, and make new friends. The social network will provide the opportunity to create personal profiles, adjust the status value, as well as use various functions such as chats, timer, news feeds, etc. Each user has the opportunity to find new acquaintances, read other people's posts. A distinctive feature of our social network is the Pomodoro timer, which allows users not to be distracted while working and competently schedule their plans. Only registered users can use our social network, which means that all users can be sure of the complete confidentiality of their data.
